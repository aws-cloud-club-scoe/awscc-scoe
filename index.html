Club Overview

Our club focuses on teaching AWS Cloud technologies by combining rigorous technical training and real-world project experience. We believe in learning by doing, and our events and workshops are designed to offer you a taste of the challenges and excitement in the world of cloud computing.

Every workshop is carefully curated to help you build industry-ready skills. Whether you’re new to AWS or looking to upgrade your skills, our club provides a supportive environment and expert guidance.
